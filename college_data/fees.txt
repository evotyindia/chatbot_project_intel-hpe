SECTION 7: SCHOLARSHIPS AND FEE CONCESSIONS

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MERIT-BASED SCHOLARSHIPS

1. Top Rank Holders (Entrance Exam):
   - Rank 1: 50% tuition fee waiver
   - Rank 2-3: 40% tuition fee waiver
   - Rank 4-10: 30% tuition fee waiver

2. JEE Main (for B.Tech students):
   - Rank below 5,000: 40% scholarship
   - Rank 5,001-10,000: 30% scholarship
   - Rank 10,001-20,000: 20% scholarship

3. CAT/MAT/CMAT (for MBA students):
   - CAT Percentile 90+: 40% scholarship
   - CAT Percentile 80-90: 30% scholarship
   - MAT/CMAT percentile 85+: 25% scholarship

4. 12th Board Examination:
   - 95%+: 30% tuition fee waiver
   - 90-95%: 25% tuition fee waiver
   - 85-90%: 15% tuition fee waiver

SPORTS SCHOLARSHIP:

- National Level Players: 30% tuition fee waiver
- State Level Players: 20% tuition fee waiver
- District Level Players: 10% tuition fee waiver

Documents Required:
- Sports achievement certificates
- Recommendation from coach/sports authority

SPECIAL CATEGORY SCHOLARSHIPS:

1. Defence Personnel Ward:
   - 20% tuition fee concession
   - For wards of serving/retired armed forces personnel
   - Documents: Service certificate, relation proof

2. Single Girl Child:
   - 15% tuition fee concession
   - Documents: Affidavit from parents

3. Economically Weaker Section (EWS):
   - Up to 25% fee concession
   - Based on family income
   - Documents: Income certificate (below INR 4 lakh/year)

4. Girl Students:
   - 10% concession on hostel fees
   - Applicable to all girl students

CATEGORY-BASED GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS:

SC/ST/OBC Scholarships:
- Post-Matric Scholarship
- Apply through National Scholarship Portal
- Amount: As per government norms
- Directly credited to student account

Documents Required:
- Category certificate
- Income certificate
- Bank account details
- Aadhaar card

NEED-BASED FINANCIAL AID:

For students facing genuine financial hardship:
- Application to Scholarship Committee
- Income proof required (family income below INR 3 lakh)
- Academic merit considered (minimum 60%)
- Up to 30% fee concession

SIBLING CONCESSION:

- Two siblings studying simultaneously: 5% each
- Three or more siblings: 10% each

MERIT-CUM-MEANS SCHOLARSHIP:

- For students excelling academically but facing financial constraints
- GPA above 8.5 + family income below INR 5 lakh
- 20-30% fee concession

SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION PROCESS:

1. Apply online through student portal
2. Submit documents to Scholarship Office
3. Committee reviews applications
4. Approval within 30 days
5. Scholarship adjusted in semester fee

Important Notes:
- Scholarships applicable only on tuition fees
- Not applicable on registration, examination, or other fees
- Maintained every year based on academic performance (minimum 75%)
- Multiple scholarships cannot be clubbed (highest applicable)

SECTION 8: REFUND POLICY

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FEE REFUND RULES:

ADMISSION CANCELLATION BEFORE SEMESTER STARTS:

Timeline | Refund Percentage
---------|------------------
Before 60 days | 90% refund (minus processing fee)
45-60 days before | 80% refund
30-45 days before | 70% refund
15-30 days before | 50% refund
Less than 15 days | 25% refund
After semester starts | No refund

Non-Refundable Components:
- Registration fee
- Caution deposit (refunded at program completion)
- Application fee
- Processing charges (INR 5,000)

WITHDRAWAL DURING SEMESTER:

- No tuition fee refund once semester begins
- Other fees (exam, library, etc.) may be refunded pro-rata
- Hostel fees refunded for unused months (minus 1 month notice)

HOSTEL FEE REFUND:

- Before occupancy: 100% refund (minus deposit)
- Within 15 days of occupancy: 75% refund
- Within 30 days: 50% refund
- After 30 days: Pro-rata refund for remaining months (1 month notice required)
- Security deposit: Refunded after final clearance

REFUND PROCESS:

1. Submit written application to Accounts Department
2. Attach original fee receipts
3. No Dues Certificate from all departments
4. Refund processed within 45 working days
5. Refund via NEFT to registered bank account

EXCEPTIONS:

- Medical emergency (with medical certificate): Special consideration
- Natural calamity: Case-by-case review
- University fault: Full refund
