FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

Q1: Is JEE Main mandatory for B.Tech admission?
A: JEE Main is preferred but not mandatory. Students can also appear for NCU Entrance Test.

Q2: Can I apply for multiple programs?
A: Yes, separate applications required for each program.

Q3: Is there any age limit for admission?
A: No age limit for UG programs except for reserved category relaxations.

Q4: When will I receive my admit card?
A: Admit cards are issued 7 days before entrance test/interview.

Q5: Can I get admission without entrance exam?
A: BCA, BBA, BA admit based on 12th merit. B.Tech and MBA require entrance scores.

Q6: Is hostel facility mandatory?
A: No, hostel is optional and subject to availability.

Q7: Can I change my specialization after admission?
A: Specialization change possible in first semester subject to approval and seat availability.

Q8: What is the refund policy if I withdraw?
A: Refund policy available on website. Generally, registration fee is non-refundable.
