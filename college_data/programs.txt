1. BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS (BCA)

Program Overview:
The BCA program at NCU is designed to produce skilled IT professionals
with strong programming, software development, and analytical skills.

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Intake Capacity: 120 Students per year

Eligibility Criteria:
- 10+2 with Mathematics OR Computer Science
- Minimum 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories)
- No age limit

Admission Basis:
- Merit-based on Class 12th marks
- Personal Interview
- Aptitude Test (if required)

Core Subjects (Semester-wise):

Semester 1:
- Fundamentals of Computers
- Programming in C
- Digital Electronics
- Mathematics-I
- English Communication
- Environmental Studies

Semester 2:
- Data Structures using C
- Object-Oriented Programming (C++)
- Database Management Systems
- Mathematics-II
- Business Communication
- Computer Organization

Semester 3:
- Java Programming
- Web Technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
- Operating Systems
- Software Engineering
- Computer Networks
- Elective-I

Semester 4:
- Python Programming
- Advanced Database Management
- Linux and Shell Programming
- Mobile Application Development
- E-Commerce
- Elective-II

Semester 5:
- Cloud Computing
- Data Science and Analytics
- Cyber Security
- Project Work-I
- Minor Project
- Elective-III

Semester 6:
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Major Project / Internship (6 months)
- Seminar
- Elective-IV

Elective Options:
- Big Data Analytics
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Blockchain Technology
- Digital Marketing
- Game Development
- React Native Development

Practical Components:
- 40% of curriculum is practical lab work
- Industry projects from Semester 4 onwards
- Mandatory 6-month internship in final semester
- Hackathons and coding competitions

Career Opportunities:
- Software Developer
- Web Developer

- Data Analyst

Top Recruiters:

- Startups and IT Services Companies

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PhD PROGRAMS AT THE NORTHCAP UNIVERSITY

Duration: Minimum 3 Years, Maximum 6 Years

NCU offers research programs in multiple disciplines to foster innovation
and contribute to academic knowledge.

AREAS OF RESEARCH:

1. Engineering
   - Computer Science Engineering
   - Electronics & Communication Engineering
   - Mechanical Engineering

2. Management and Business Administration
   - Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations
   - Business Analytics
   - Entrepreneurship

3. Sciences
   - Mathematics
   - Physics
   - Chemistry
   - Applied Sciences

4. Humanities and Social Sciences
   - Psychology
   - Economics
   - Education

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Eligibility Criteria:

- Master's Degree in relevant field
- Minimum 55% (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD candidates)
- NET/GATE/SLET qualified candidates given preference
- Published research papers (preferred)

Admission Process:

1. Application Submission
   - Online application
   - Research proposal (1500-2000 words)
   - Statement of purpose

2. Entrance Test
   - Written test on research methodology
   - Subject-specific test

3. Interview
   - Research committee interview
   - Presentation of research proposal
   - Subject knowledge assessment

4. Supervisor Allocation
   - Based on research area
   - Mutual consent

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Program Structure:

Year 1: Coursework
- Research Methodology
- Advanced topics in specialization
- Literature Review
- Comprehensive Examination

Year 2-3: Research Work
- Data collection
- Experiments/Surveys
- Analysis
- Paper publications
- Pre-submission seminar
- Thesis writing

Year 3+: Thesis Submission and Defense
- Thesis submission
- External examiner review
- Viva voce (Oral defense)
- Degree award

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Facilities for PhD Scholars:

- Dedicated research labs
- Access to online journals and databases
- Research grants for projects
- Conference participation support
- National and international collaboration opportunities

Financial Support:

Full-time PhD Scholars:
- Monthly Fellowship: INR 31,000 (first 2 years)
- Monthly Fellowship: INR 35,000 (next 3 years)
- Contingency grant for research

Part-time PhD:
- No fellowship
- Flexible research schedule

Registration and Fees:
- Registration Fee: INR 50,000 (One-time)
- Annual Fee: INR 1,20,000

Publication Requirement:
- Minimum 2 research papers in Scopus/UGC-approved journals
- Mandatory before thesis submission

Career Outcomes:
- Faculty positions in universities/colleges
- Research positions in industry
- Postdoctoral research
- R&D roles in corporates
- Government research institutions
